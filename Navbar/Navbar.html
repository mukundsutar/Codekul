Humans co-exist with creatures known as Pokémon, a large variety of species endowed with special powers. Pokémon are found all over the world of Pokémon, and their habitat varies depending on the species, much like creatures in the real world.

The relationship between a Pokémon and a human varies, depending on the species and personality. In the world of Pokémon, several humans become Pokémon Trainers, who capture Pokémon to then keep them as a traditional pet or to have them engage in Pokémon battles.
